Getting Around

Transportation

Hotel Perk 🚌
Complimentary Shuttle Service
The Rose offers a complimentary shuttle within a 3-mile radius of the hotel — and this is a big one. That range covers all of the Friday and Saturday evening events, plus CTA and Metra stops that connect you straight into the city. Whether you want to explore Chicago's museums, catch the Air & Water Show, or just wander, the shuttle makes it easy to get out and back without needing a car. It also runs to and from the airport, so if you're flying in, you're covered from the moment you land.
